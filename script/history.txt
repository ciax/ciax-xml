# Stream Log Format
2010 Ver1-3
2011-12 Ver4-5
2013- Ver6
Ver   Format
0     NNNNNNNNNN.NNNNN \t dir:cmd(:par) \t dump
1     NNNNNNNNNN.NNN \t dir:cmd(:par) \t dump
2     NNNNNNNNNN.NNN \t id:dir(ver):cmd(:par) \t dump
3     NNNNNNNNNN.NNN \t id:dir:cmd(:par) \t dump
4     NNNNNNNNNN.NNN \t id:ver:dir:cmd(:par) \t base64
5     NNNNNNNNNN.NNN \t JSON{id,ver,dir,cmd:par,base64}
6     JSON{id:"NNNNNNNNNN.NNN",ver,cmd,par,dir,base64}
7     JSON{id:"NNNNNNNNNNNNN",ver,cmd,par,dir,base64}
